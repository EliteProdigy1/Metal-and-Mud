From the Studio

Built by Hand.
Fired with Intention.

Every mug, plate, and tumbler is shaped and painted by hand before it ever sees the kiln — no molds, no shortcuts. Eyes, serpents, and chains are drawn freehand in ink-black slip and blood-red glaze, then fired until the story sets for good. Small batches. No two the same.
